<p><strong>Student Hub Services</strong>
</p>
<p>The Student Hub brings together our School Counsellors, Special Educational
Needs (SEN) Officers and Education and Career Guidance (ECG) Counsellor
to support students’ well-being, learning and growth. We provide a safe
space for students who may need help with their mental health, social-emotional
development, behavioral concerns or friendship issues.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><u>How We Support Students</u>
</p>
<p>•&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Counselling
and emotional support</p>
<p>•&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Social
and behavioral guidance</p>
<p>•&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; SEN
intervention and learning support</p>
<p>•&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Individual
or small-group check-ins</p>
<p>•&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Education
and Career Guidance (ECG) through counselling, coaching and workshops to
support informed pathway planning towards post-secondary education institutions.</p>
