<p>With effect from 2022, all student accident insurance claims submission
are to be submitted electronically.&nbsp; Parents/students are to submit
their claims online and check the claims status through the online portal.
All claims should be submitted as soon as possible subjected to a cap of
365 days from the date of accident. &nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
